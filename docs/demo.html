Flip a Coin, Fund a Cause

Pick heads or tails. Win the flip and $0.01 USDC is donated to charity automatically via the x402 protocol. No wallet needed — the server donates on your behalf.

This demo shows how any app can embed automatic charitable giving into user actions. The donation is paid by the app owner's server wallet, not the user.
