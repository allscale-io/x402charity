Who Can Use x402 Charity?

Any product with a backend can add automatic giving in under 10 lines of code.

DeFi / Trading

DEX & Trading Platforms

Donate a fraction of a cent every time a user places a trade. With millions of swaps per day, even $0.0001 per trade adds up to thousands of dollars for charity — and your traders feel good about every order.

Market it as: "Every swap you make helps fund clean water."

user places trade → $0.0001 donated 
// Express middleware on your trade endpoint
app.use('/api/swap', x402charity({
  privateKey: process.env.KEY,
  cause: 'clean-water',
  amount: '0.0001',
  shouldDonate: (req) =>
    req.method === 'POST',
}));
10K trades / day
$1 donated / day
$365 / year auto
Prediction Markets

Betting & Prediction Platforms

Every bet placed triggers a micro-donation. Turn speculation into social impact. Users can see the total charitable impact of the platform's activity — transforming gambling into a net positive.

Market it as: "Win or lose, every bet makes a difference."

user places bet → $0.001 donated 
// Next.js middleware for bet endpoints
export default x402charity({
  privateKey: process.env.KEY,
  cause: 'education-fund',
  amount: '0.001',
  matcher: '/api/bet/*',
});
